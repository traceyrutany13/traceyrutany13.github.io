説明: Product Introduction
SmallRig x LensVid mini L-Shaped Mount Plate Kit is co-designed by Iddo Genuth, co-founder and editor of LensVid.com, a photography and video website that has been in business for 10 years, and SmallRig DreamRig program. This kit provides a more compact solution for mounting equipment and accessories, such as wireless image transmitters, monitors, V-mount plates, etc., at a 90° angle to your camera. It also comes with four mounting solutions: 1/4"-20 screw, 1/4"-20 locating screw, 3/8"-16 screw, and 3/8""-16 locating hole for ARRI to fulfill more usage scenarios for our users. The device features a built-in anti-slip silicone pad that protects your camera from scratches and ensures secure mounting. The load-bearing capacity of 1.2kg/2.6lb is compatible with most wireless image transmitters and accessories.
