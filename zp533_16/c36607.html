説明:

Benefit from 8 hours of continuous audio enjoyment and unparalleled sonic fidelity with the Beatnik Bluetooth speaker from Knox Gear. It features TWS (True Wireless Stereo) technology that enables pairing to other Beatnik speakers. Create an impromptu surround sound system for watching movies or jam out to your favorite tracks as if you were listening live in the front row.

Product Features

  • TWS Pairing
  • IPX5 Waterproof
  • AUX-In Cable
  • microSD Slot
  • USB-C Input Charging
  • Available Colors: White, Black, Blue

Pair to Other Beatnik Speakers

Connect via Bluetooth 5.0 from any smartphone, tablet, laptop, or Bluetooth-enabled audio device. Engineered with leading TWS (True Wireless Stereo) technology that allows you to pair two or more Beatnik speakers, meaning that you can transmit the L channel (left) and the R channel (right) separately. Link up with additional Beatnik speakers for a daisy-chain effect that boosts audio and expands the sound range further.

Wet Without Worry

IPX5 waterproof design can be indirectly splashed, rained on, or hit with a low-pressure stream of water safely. Perfect for pool parties, beach trips, and singing in the shower. USB-C input means faster charging times compared to USB 3.0.

Multiple Ways to Connect

Connect to laptops, smartphones, and tablets using a Bluetooth 5.0 connection that extends to a range of 100 ft. You can also connect to a personal computer, TV, or other non-Bluetooth devices via the 3.5mm aux-in jack.
