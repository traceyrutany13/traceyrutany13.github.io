    1. Only support 2.4GHz wifi, not 5GHz.
    2. Wifi password less than 31 words, no special characters "=".
    3. No more than 5 meters from camera, no wall between camera and router.
    4. If fail in connection, please reset and try again when indicator flashes red.
    5. For the first connection and wifi paring, the camera and phone connect the same 2.4Ghz wifi.

    【Micro SD Card Tips】
    1. Micro SD card above class 6.
    2. Insert/remove the formatted card when out of power.
    3. Check the playback after 6 minutes because each video is recorded in 5 minutes.
    4. If the camera can’t recognize SD card, format card with computer by choosing FAT32.
