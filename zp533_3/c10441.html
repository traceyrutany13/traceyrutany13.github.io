[楽譜] ツール・ド・フォース (ジェイガー) 吹奏楽譜【送料無料】(Tour de Force)《輸入楽譜》

ジャンル：オリジナル(輸入)

出版社：Wingert-Jones

弊社に在庫がない場合の取り寄せ発送目安：8日〜31日

作曲者：Robert Jager／ロバート・ジェイガー

グレード：3

編成：吹奏楽

演奏時間：2分20秒

解説：Looking for an exciting， different but still traditional setting of a march ? Then you and your students will enjoy this original work composed in 1973 for a foreign tour band. Both woodwind and brass writing is playable for good junior high bands as well as high school groups. Ranges are moderate， rhythms tricky but within the grade level of the work. (2 minutes 30 seconds)
