説明: ensure that the valuable carbon steel knife is unharmed and lasts longer： 1. When you buy a brand new knife and bring it home, you can perform the "opening ceremony" before using it (optional). 2. Before using the carbon steel knife for the first time, prepare a paper towel, dip it in an appropriate amount of white vinegar and use it to wipe the entire blade, then let it sit in 1 minute. 3. Rinse off the white vinegar on the knife with water, dry the blade thoroughly, then repeat the action of wiping with white vinegar, the whole procedure should be carried out 2 to 3 times. 4. In the process of use, the carbon steel knife will begin to appear "green rust", the blade appears a blue-gray color. 5. "Green rust" is not the meaning of rust, but rather a layer of protection, can slow down the speed of oxidation, rust. 6. As the blade grows in use, the color of the "green rust" will deepen, becoming its own beautiful historical mark. 7. Before use: carbon steel knives are quite easy to sharpen it is one of its characteristics. The correct way to use is to sharpen the knife before each use (daily use is not necessary). 8. the reason why the knife can not be cleanly cut to the end, may be the knife blade is bent, so when sharpening the knife must pay special attention to, it is recommended to use a sharpening stone rather than a sharpening rod. 9. After each cut food, dry the blade with a clean kitchen towel. 10. Especially when handling acidic food, the action of "wipe dry" is indispensable. 11. After handling the ingredients, do not leave the knives lying around, dry them, not throw them into the pile of dishes in the sink to soak, or put them in the dishwasher. 12. The right thing to do is to immediately wash the knives thoroughly with warm water and dishwashing detergent. 13. After cleaning, dry the blade with a clean cloth and wrap the knife in a cloth before putting it in a drawer, not leave it "naked" and unprotected in the cabinet.
